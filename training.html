Training Programs at C.D. Tools Inc.

At C.D. Tools Inc., we offer a variety of training programs designed to help you master the use of our tools. Whether you are a professional contractor or a DIY enthusiast, our training sessions provide valuable knowledge and hands-on experience with our products.

Our Training Courses Include:

  • Introduction to Power Tools
  • Advanced Sawing Techniques
  • Safety Practices for Tool Use
  • Tool Maintenance and Care

Training Locations

Our training sessions are available at our main office in Sarnia, ON, as well as at various industry events and trade shows. Check our Media page for upcoming event announcements.

How to Register

To sign up for one of our training sessions, please contact us at support@cdtools.com or call us at +1 (519) 123-1234. You can also fill out the contact form on our Contact Us page.
