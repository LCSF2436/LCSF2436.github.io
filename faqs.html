Frequently Asked Questions

What types of saws does C.D. Tools Inc. offer?

We offer a wide range of electric saws, including circular saws, jigsaws, reciprocating saws, and more. These tools are designed for both professional and home use.

Do you offer warranties on your products?

Yes, we provide warranties on all of our products. The specific warranty period depends on the type of product. Please check the product details for more information.

Can I order products online?

Currently, we do not offer direct online purchasing. However, you can contact us to place an order, and we can arrange for delivery or pickup.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@cdtools.com or by calling +1(519)-123-1234.
